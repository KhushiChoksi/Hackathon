Grade 2

The different content information that is taught and the main learning concepts:

1. Math

- Numbers up to 100 - Benchmarks of 25, 50 ,and 100 - Addition and subtraction to 100 - Direct linear measurements - Concrete graphs

2. English

- Building on elements of a story such as characters and plot - Learning the structure of compound sentences - Features of oral language such as tone, volume and pace

3. Science

- Metamorphic and non-metamorphic life cycles - Types of forces - Physical and chemical attributes of changing materials - Water cycle, water sources and water conservation - Similarities and differences of parents and offspring

4. Social Studies

- How people's need and wants are met by the community - Relationships between people/communities and how they interact with the environment - Rights and responsibilites of individuals regionally and globaly
