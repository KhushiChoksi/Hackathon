Grade 1

The different content information that is taught and the main learning concepts:

1. Math

-Learning numbers up to 20 - Addding and subtraction up to 20 - 2D and 3D shapes - Meaning of equality and inequality - Value of coins and monetary exchanges

2. English

-Elements of a story - Vocabulary regarding texts - Sentence structure - Punctuation use

3. Science

- Classification of living and non-living things - Structural features of living things in the environment ( plants with roots and stems) - Natural and artificial sources of light - Common objects in the sky - Behaviors of animals such as nesting, hibernation

4. Social Studies

- Characteristics of local community and that focus on organizations and community needs - Diverse cultures, backgrounds and perspectives - Relationships between community and its environment
